About Us

Tita Fridas Pizza and Shawarma established year 2018. Its name "Fridas" came from the mother's name of chief Gilbert the owner of store. Our first branch is located in Poblacion Arayat,Pampanga. After three years chief gilbert decided to expand his business by build on another branch in Sta.ana and Mexico Pampanga.
